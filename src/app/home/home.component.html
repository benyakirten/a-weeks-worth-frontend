A Week's Worth

A way to translate recipes, keep track of your meals and craft a shopping list for a week, all in one place

Register an account and dive into the fun

As soon as you join, you can customize your shopping for yourself, or join with the other members of your household in a group and start planning out your meals in one, centralized place.

Why do this and not just write stuff down like a normal human being?

I can't answer that for you, but I can tell you why I designed this website. Halfway to get practice with GraphQL and Angular, but halfway so I wouldn't have to do this anymore for my household. This way everything is in a central repository that's evident to everyone.

What are some of the caveats?

Putting any information on the internet has inherent risk. That said, the database that keeps your information is decently safe. A few things to keep in mind, though. Who is in which group is publicly available information, and all recipes are shared among all members. Any member of a group has complete control over every aspect of a group.
