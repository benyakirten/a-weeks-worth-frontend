About

Why does this site exist?

The idea of this website is that you and/or your family can organize your week's meals and shopping in one place. I guess it's in the place of having a whiteboard in the kitchen in something. This way you can consume precious resources like electricity, computing resources or bandwith on this website instead of, like, writing things down physically? With your own hand? Gross. I guess it can do some nifty things like tabulate different ingredients together. It also exists so I could practice what I'd learned about Django, GraphQL and Angular.

How do I use this site?

Before you do anything, you can look at what recipes are available without making an account. Likewise, you can browse this page and the privacy policy without making one. But to do anything else, you will need one. Go to login/register and make an account (or log in with an account you already have but make sure it's on login mode, that only has two boxes to fill out instead of 4). Then you have a choice of two things: 1. setting up your groups and/or joining one. You go to the groups page, and from there you can make a request to join a group or make one yourself. Once you've done joined/made your groups, you can see if anyone wants to join any of your groups or their members by clicking on the box that says "my groups". You (or any of their members) can also disband any of your groups at any time. 2. To get started on your shopping list, click on 'the week'. From the dropdown menu you can look at any of your groups or just your own personal shopping list. Once you're viewing it, if you click 'edit', you can set up a shopping list and your plans for your whole week (or just the days you want).
