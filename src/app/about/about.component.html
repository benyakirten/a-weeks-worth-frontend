About

Why does this site exist?

The idea of this website is that you and/or your family can organize your week's meals and shopping in one place. I guess it's in the place of having a whiteboard in the kitchen in something. This way you can consume precious resources like electricity, computing resources or bandwith on this website instead of, like, writing things down physically? With your own hand? Gross. I guess it can do some nifty things like tabulate different ingredients together. It also exists so I could practice what I'd learned about Django, GraphQL and Angular.

How do I use this site?

Sign up for an account under Login/Register. Then you can get started under The Week. If you want, you can join a group/make one/request entry to one and coordinate with other people. You can join in an unlimited number of groups, though I have no idea why you'd want to. You can use r2api to translate select recipes from Italian to English, too. I'll update this when I've designed all those parts.

Who am I?

My name is Benyakir Horowitz. I am a writer and editor who has spent the last year learning programming. You can check out my blog or my Github to learn more about me and what I do. Or you can write me a message using the boxes below. It sends an email to my personal email account. Cool, eh?
